### About the Lab

The TRACE Lab at the University of Cambridge is a research group focused on building trustworthy artificial intelligence (AI) systems for high-stakes settings, like healthcare, education, and public safety. We develop new algorithms and tools that support effective human-AI interaction and manage the partnership between humans and AI agents.
